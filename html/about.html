What is Factory Head?

Factory Head is a project that will help users increase their Pokemon Platinum Battle Factory Win Rate. Factory Head allows you to get a full picture of the opponent's Pokemon. By a full picture, I mean

  • Species
  • Typing
  • Base Stats
  • EVs
  • Move Set
  • Item
  • Nature
  • Ability

Motivation

Pokemon Platinum Battle Factory is one of my favorite games because of the randomization with which I choose my team. First, I do not have to perfectly grind out making a level 100 team. Second, I have to make a team that I think will work well against my opponent given a limited number of options. In this sense, it reminds of Arena mode in the Hearthstone card game, which for the same reason stated above is one of my favorite games. I made this out of desire to get the Gold Print in Battle Factory which is obtained after 49 consecutive wins. I once reached 41 wins, but lost due to a lack of knowledge.
